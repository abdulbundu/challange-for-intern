Meet The Philly Rescue Ferrets Team

Our founder Dolph Lundgren, shown here educating a class of disadvantaged children about the importance of ferret grooming, is a genius and revolutionary zoologist. He was the first person to correctly idetify and name the 'Chinese' species of ferret. He has devoted his life to helping his furry friends. We thank him for his guidence.

Zach Feldman is a consummate ferret-ologist and has been a ferret-er from childhood. He is a Co-Founder and currently Chief Cuddle Officer of Philly Rescue Ferrets, devising population control strategies, hiring ferret wranglers, and instructing adopters. He wrangles ferrets using ropes, nets, traps, blankets, and salad-tongs.
